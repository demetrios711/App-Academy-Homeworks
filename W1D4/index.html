Gyro

Ingredients

  • Lamb, Beef or Pork in Rotisserie Form
  • Potatoes
  • Pita Bread
  • Tzatziki
  • Tomato
  • Onion

Description

Meat in a piece of pita bread with french fries

Directions

  1. Cook meat on Rotisserie, slice into thin slices.
  2. Fry french fries
  3. Place all ingredients into pita bread
